# AI Ethics and Responsible AI
Ethical considerations, frameworks, and guidelines for developing and deploying AI systems responsibly.

## Topics Covered
- Ethical principles for AI development
- Bias detection and mitigation
- Fairness, accountability, and transparency
- AI governance and regulation
- Privacy and data protection
- Human rights in AI applications

## Resources

- [Ethics of AI - University of Helsinki](https://ethics-of-ai.mooc.fi) - Free self-paced online course exploring what AI ethics means and how to develop AI ethically. Covers practical cases including data usage, algorithmic decisions, and human rights considerations.
- [AI Ethics at SAP (OpenSAP)](https://open.sap.com/courses/ai1) - Free practical course on developing an ethical AI framework and implementing it in practice. Suitable for professionals and beginners.
- [AI Governance and Ethics by Alison](https://alison.com/course/ai-governance-and-ethics) - Free CPD-accredited course exploring laws, frameworks, and ethical challenges in AI. Includes security and compliance considerations.
- [Ethics of Artificial Intelligence (Coursera)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ethics-of-artificial-intelligence) - Free course (audit mode) dealing with ethical issues created by AI. Uses case studies and ethical frameworks to analyze AI impact.
- [AI Safety, Ethics and Society by Center for AI Safety](https://course.aisafetyfundamentals.com/) - Free 12-week instructor-led course on AI safety and ethics. Includes optional AI fundamentals week and personal project phase.
